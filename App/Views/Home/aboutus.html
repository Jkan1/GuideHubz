AboutUs

The project is a website with an objective of providing support to the users for preparing for the popular government as well as private sector examinations. The website also acts as an Education Purpose website which is developed by the group members.

The primary goal of the website is to reduce unemployment and provide support with sufficient information as well as content related to vacancies of government as well as private sector like UPSC, SSC, PPSC, DELHI POLICE, PUNJAB POLICE, INDIAN RAILWAYS, UPPSC, MPPSC, GATE, AFCAT, CDS, OTA, and different private jobs also.
